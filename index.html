Built for Grow a Garden traders

Welcome, gardeners. Grow a Garden has a lot of plants, pets and event items, and it is easy to feel unsure when trading for something you have never seen before. Prices can move with seasons, updates and player hype, so TradePulse is here as a quick reference for comparing offers, checking whether a deal looks fair, and giving players a place to discuss values together. Use the numbers as a starting point, negotiate when needed, and let the community vote on real W/F/L trades.

Because trading is a free market, no price list can be absolutely correct forever. TradePulse uses player votes, comments and reports as extra signals, and the mutation calculator helps gardeners estimate mutated crop value before a trade.

TradePulse is completely free to use. If it helps you, bookmark it and share it with your friends. Feedback from gardeners is always welcome. To protect your account and items, never share personal information, account details or game passwords with anyone on this site.
