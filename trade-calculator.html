Compare both sides of a Grow a Garden trade, see whether it looks like a Win, Fair or Loss, then share it for community votes.

Start a trade check

W

Win means the other offer is worth more than your offer.

F

Fair means both sides are close enough that comments may decide.

L

Loss means your offer appears higher value than what you receive.
