Start a trade check

W

Win means the other offer is worth more than your offer, so the trade may be good for you.

F

Fair means both sides are close enough that comments, demand and item trend may decide.

L

Loss means your offer appears higher value than what you receive, so check twice before accepting.

What can the calculator compare?

TradePulse supports Grow a Garden pets, crops, seeds and gear. Players can compare names such as Fairy, Kitsune, Disco Bee, Fennec Fox, Raccoon, Eggsnapper, Omniblossom, Infinite Prism, Cosmic Lotus, Magic Bean and Rainbow Hoe.

Why community voting matters

A raw value number is not always enough. Demand, hype, recent updates and trader behavior can change whether a deal feels like a W, F or L. TradePulse lets players post a trade to the community board, collect votes, read comments and track feedback from their own profile page.
