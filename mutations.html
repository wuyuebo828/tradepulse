Mutation Formula

Final value = base crop value x weight^2 x Variant multiplier x Environmental multipliers

Example: 100 base x Rainbow 50x x Shocked 100x x 2kg weight^2 = 2,000,000 Sheckles.

Variant mutations vs Environmental mutations

Variant mutations are single-choice states such as Ripe, Silver, Gold and Rainbow. Environmental mutations can stack, so a crop can combine effects like Wet, Chilled, Moonlit, Shocked, Disco, Voidtouched or Abyssal.

Why mutations matter

Grow a Garden crop trades can change dramatically when rare mutations stack. TradePulse separates base crop values from mutation multipliers so players can estimate a trade before posting it for community W/F/L votes.

The mutation calculator is designed for gardeners who want to estimate mutation value before buying, selling or negotiating. It combines base crop value, weight and mutation multipliers, then gives a quick result that can be compared with community trade opinions.

Mutation FAQ

Can Environmental mutations stack? Yes. TradePulse multiplies selected Environmental mutations together.

Does crop weight matter? Yes. The calculator uses weight squared, so heavier mutated crops can become much more valuable.

Are mutation multipliers official? No. Treat them as fan-made estimates and verify with community trades.

Why can mutation prices change? Mutation value is affected by free trading, rarity, season demand and how much players want that crop at the moment.
