Grow a Garden Summer Camp 2026 Event Guide NEW

The Summer Camp event launched June 6, 2026 with 4 brand-new pets available from Campfire Eggs. Here is every pet, its ability, how to get it, and what it is worth on the market right now.

What is the Summer Camp 2026 event?

Summer Camp is a limited-time Grow a Garden event that started June 6, 2026. Players can earn Campfire Eggs through event activities and hatch them for exclusive pets. There are also Premium Campfire Eggs with better drop rates for the rarer pets. The event introduced 4 pets, each with unique abilities that interact with crop growth, mutations, and trading.

Prices change daily. The values below are from TradePulse data as of mid-June 2026. Check the live value list for current prices before trading.

1. Cicada NEW

Rarity: Event Egg: Campfire Egg Drop Rate: 55% Hunger: 2,000

Ability — Summer Buzz: Every 7:20 to 3:60 minutes, Cicada lands on a plant and accelerates its growth in a burst. Simple but effective for speeding up harvest cycles.

Trading notes: Cicada is the most common Summer Camp pet at 55% drop rate from Campfire Eggs. Expect low trade value due to high supply, but it is useful as a utility pet for active farmers who want faster crop cycles. Good entry-level event pet for new players.

2. Newt NEW

Rarity: Event Egg: Campfire Egg Drop Rate: 35.5% Hunger: 10,000

Ability — Renewtal / Summer Harvester: Each harvest has an 8-16% chance to duplicate the harvested fruit. For Summer-type fruit specifically, the duplication chance gets an extra 5-10% boost from the Summer Harvester passive.

Trading notes: Newt has strong synergy with Summer-type crops. If you are farming seasonal or event crops, Newt effectively doubles your output over time. Moderate trade value — players who understand crop duplication will pay more. A smart hold for patient traders.

3. Nightjar NEW

Rarity: Event Egg: Premium Campfire Egg Drop Rate: 9% Hunger: 10,000

Ability — Call of the Night: Every 10:00 to 5:00 minutes, Nightjar consumes a Moonlit fruit and grants a Small XP boost plus a Small Passive boost to a random pet. If the fruit has BOTH Bloodlit and Moonlit mutations, the boost becomes a Large Passive boost instead. Ignores favorited crops, so plan your garden layout accordingly.

Trading notes: Only 9% drop rate from the Premium egg makes Nightjar significantly rarer than Cicada and Newt. The Bloodlit+Moonlit combo requirement adds strategy depth. Higher trade value than the common drops. Worth targeting if you run Moonlit mutations regularly.

4. Fire Wisp NEW

Rarity: Event Egg: Premium / Rainbow Premium Drop Rate: 0.5% Hunger: 50,000

Ability 1 — Heart of Fire: Every 10:00 to 5:00 minutes, 5-10% chance to apply the Ember crop variant to a random fruit.

Ability 2 — Fireheart: Every 18:00 to 10:00 minutes, targets a Level 100 pet with no mutation. Success resets the pet to Level 1 and grants the Ember pet mutation (+32% passive boost + 32% XP boost). Failure resets it to Level 50. This is a high-risk, high-reward ability unique to Fire Wisp.

Trading notes: At 0.5% drop rate, Fire Wisp is the rarest Summer Camp pet and the hardest to obtain. The Fireheart ability is one of the most powerful pet mutation sources in the game if it succeeds, but failing costs 50 levels. High trade value due to extreme rarity and unique Ember mutation access. Expect premium prices on the trade market.

Should you open Campfire Eggs or trade?

Campfire Eggs have decent rates for the common pets (55% Cicada, 35.5% Newt). If you just want one of each for your collection, opening eggs is fine. But if you want Nightjar or Fire Wisp, the 9% and 0.5% rates mean you could open dozens of Premium eggs without seeing one. Trading may be cheaper in the long run.

Fire Wisp specifically is a candidate for direct purchase on the trade market. At 0.5% drop rate from a Premium egg, the expected cost in eggs is far higher than most trade prices will be once the market settles.

FAQ

When does Summer Camp 2026 end?
Rockstar has not announced an end date yet. Limited-time events typically run 2-4 weeks, so act before it ends if you want the event-exclusive pets.

Can I still get Fire Wisp after the event ends?
If Fire Wisp is event-exclusive, it will no longer be obtainable from eggs after Summer Camp ends. Existing pets remain in inventories and can be traded. This usually drives prices up after the event closes.

Are Summer Camp pets worth investing in?
Nightjar and Fire Wisp have strong abilities and low supply. If the event ends without returning, their trade value could increase. Cicada and Newt have higher supply and will likely stay affordable.
